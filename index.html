Captain Paker's Pub


Lambakin, Marilao


Captain Parker's is a nautically themed bar and fish house that won chowder competitions so often that is has been forced to retire from entry.



Cle Elum Bakery


Bocaue, Bulacan


Cake donuts and maple bars are at their best in this long-standing town bakery, which also is known for its cookies and bread. Good coffee, too.



Hank’s Hamburgers


Malolos, Bulacan


Hank’s hash-house hamburgers are good and greasy, a Tulsa best. Also on the menu: Frito chili pie, corn dogs and chocolate-covered peanut butter bon bons.



Cleve Edmunds’ Bar-B-Que


Bulakan, Bulacan


Everything on the traditional Central Savannah River Area BBQ menu is done right at Cleve Edmunds’, including chopped pork, ribs, chicken, and hash on rice.



Lucali


Meycuayan, Bulacan


A new contender in a competitive field, Brooklyn’s Lucali has foodies waiting hours in line to eat what’s hyped as New York’s best pizza.



Lyndell’s Bakery


Marilao, Bulacan


Lyndell’s of Somerville, Massachusetts is one of America’s oldest bakeries, known for dense buttercream frosting and soft sponge cakes.
